Research

As a research assistant, I've worked with Professor Ani Jayakaran at WSU's Stormwater Center and with the Gray Lab at UC Berkeley/LBNL.

Low Impact Development

Helped install, calibrate, and troubleshoot various biosystems cells, including permeable pavements, canopy infiltration sites, raingardens, mesocosms, and soil columns. Aided in the collection and analysis of artificial storm data using R and Python. Built a Shiny WebApp to analyze soil moisture data of Professor Jayakaran's mulch study.

ACTS Algorithm Integration

Studied track-finding algorithms with Professor Heather Gray's lab @ LBNL. Based on the TrackML Challenge, identified several potential algorithms to improve runtime and CPU efficiency of ATLAS. Integrated the FASTRACK cellular automata algorithm with ATLAS, achieving accuracies of over 80% for multiple tracking events.

Frameworks, Languages & Libraries: C++, Root, Docker
